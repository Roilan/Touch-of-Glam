Who am I?

As a child, I had way too much time on my hands, experimenting with anything related to hair. Initially, I wanted to become a designer. Then, I realized, "I actually don't love what I'm doing". I dropped it like a bad habit, picked up hair and makeup in high school. Senior year, I was offered an internship to be a hairstylist assistant at an NYC hair salon. I fell in love with the beauty industry and everything that it has to offer.

My name is Wendy Salinas. I work with a wide range of products including, but not limited, to MAC, Lorac, Laura Mercier, Makeup Forever and many other professional cosmetic brands.
